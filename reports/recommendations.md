# Bank-Specific Recommendations

## Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE)
- **Driver:** Fast transfers praised in 38% of reviews
- **Pain Point:** OTP delays (5% of reviews)
- **Recommendation 1:** Add SMS provider redundancy for OTP delivery
- **Recommendation 2:** Implement fingerprint authentication

## Dashen Bank
- **Driver:** Clean UI praised in 42% of reviews
- **Pain Point:** Slow loading (10% of reviews)
- **Recommendation 1:** Optimize app startup time
- **Recommendation 2:** Add loading progress indicator

## Bank of Abyssinia (BOA)
- **Driver:** Functional transfers when working
- **Pain Point:** Login failures (22% of reviews)
- **Recommendation 1:** Fix login system as highest priority
- **Recommendation 2:** Add fingerprint and bill payment features
